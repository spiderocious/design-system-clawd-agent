Date: 2026-05-11. Brief: redesign buffbyteai.xyz — current product is AI content analysis for creators (script analysis, dashboard with trending, content insights, sentiment / virality / brand / platform / timing / risk / tips, teleprompter). Current visual: classic AI-SaaS-template (sky-blue gradients, six pastel feature cards, gradient text, soft-blue progress bars). User wants B&W direction.

## Round 1 answers

- **Audience:** Mix — pro creators set the bar. Means: design for the most demanding user (pro creators / agencies); side-hustlers + marketing teams should still fit. **How to apply:** depth, exports, data density allowed where it earns its place — but never overwhelming.
- **Feel:** Warm, human, calm. Notion / Craft energy in B&W. **How to apply:** soft edges (not hard), generous whitespace, readable type, never sterile.
- **B&W literal:** B&W + semantic-only colour. Mono UI; functional colour only for meaning (positive / warn / critical) in data viz. **How to apply:** no decorative tints. Pills, chrome, nav, headings are all ink-on-paper. Colour is earned by carrying meaning.
- **Density:** Sparse — lots of air. Refined-sparse, not the current emptiness. **How to apply:** big margins, one idea per row, breathing room. But sparse with *intent* — every surface has a clear focal centre.

## Round 2 answers

- **Hero screen:** Both equally — Dashboard (daily check-in) + Analysis Result (the work). Two heroes. **How to apply:** the variation pick must consider both. Build at least one polished version of each.
- **Type:** All sans, neutral grotesk. One family. **How to apply:** Inter or Söhne or Geist family. No serif "thinking" layer; type discipline comes from tracking, scale, weight — not face variety. Mono only for numbers/IDs/tickers.
- **AI voice:** Coach — encouraging but direct. Constructive, second-person, no fluff. **How to apply:** copy throughout the analysis surfaces speaks *to* the creator ("Your hook lands. Tighten the middle."). Not "Hook strength: 76%" alone — there's a sentence next to it.
- **Teleprompter:** Stay distinct — performance tool, own rules. **How to apply:** can still share family with the rest, but fullscreen / oversized type / different chrome rules are fine.

## Stance direction (synthesised)

Warm-mono, generous, semantic-colour-only, single neutral grotesk, coach-voiced AI, signature surfaces split between Dashboard and Analysis Result, teleprompter is its own thing.
