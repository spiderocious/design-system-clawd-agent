— What Irisi does —

Three steps from prompt to applause.

01 · Direct

You write the brief.

One sentence is enough. Irisi will ask three or four follow-up questions if it needs to — never more.

02 · Director

Irisi picks the stance.

Twenty-five slide packs in the library — editorial, Swiss, showroom, terminal, riso, blueprint. Each one a complete worldview, not a theme.

03 · Delivered

Present, edit, export.

Drag-rearrange, tweak the wording, swap the pack. Then present from the browser or download as .pptx / .pdf.
