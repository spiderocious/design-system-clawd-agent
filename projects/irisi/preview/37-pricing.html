Questions, plainly answered.

01 Is the React library really open-source?

Yes. The slide-rendering primitives, all 25 packs, and the Code Connect templates are MIT-licensed. The hosted app (generation, real-time, brand library, exports) is the commercial product.

02 Can I use it with my own AI provider?

On Studio, yes — bring your own keys for OpenAI, Anthropic, or self-hosted models. The function-calling schema is published per layout so any tool-calling LLM can drive Irisi directly.

03 What happens if I cancel?

Your decks stay accessible read-only forever. Exports continue to work. You drop back to Free at the next billing date — no data loss, no hostage-taking.

04 Are students really free, or "free with limits"?

Genuinely free. We expanded the Free tier to 10 decks per month for verified students & teachers — write to edu@irisi.app.

05 Can I see a custom stance built for my brand?

On Studio, our team co-designs a 26th stance with you — same depth as the catalogue, your worldview. Typical engagement is six weeks.
