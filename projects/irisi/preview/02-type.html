Type

Foundation · Three families with deliberate roles
— A composed page

Type does the heavy lifting.

Bricolage Grotesque carries the impact — display weights at 144 pixels feel like a printed magazine cover, not a website. Inter handles every chrome surface — button labels, list rows, settings copy. JetBrains Mono is reserved for record numbers, codes, hex values, and AI prompts, where the tnum feature gives every digit equal width.

The serif (Instrument / Newsreader) appears only in italic — a deliberate restraint. It marks asides, kickers, and the occasional pull quote. Not a body face. Not a substitute for sans.
