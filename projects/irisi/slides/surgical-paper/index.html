The quiet rebellion of measured growth.

Dictated by · the founders · 14 May 2026 · 09:42

Most companies confuse motion for progress. We've spent three quarters undoing that, and the numbers — finally — agree. Revenue is up forty-two percent without a single new hire on the go-to-market team.

The reason isn't strategy. It's the unglamorous work of saying no, on time, every quarter, to the dozen things we could have done but chose not to.
