Density

Two densities: editorial for the Simulator, briefing pages, and exports — wide gutters, more whitespace, the AI copilot card spaced like a newspaper column. Instrument for the War Room and the constituency map — rows tightened to 36px, mono-prefix inputs, the right-rail incident feed on a 12px gutter. Both share the same tokens; only the gutter and the row height change.
