Three takes on Soft Scandinavian — pick one

All three share the stance: warm pale neutrals, no pure black, light-only, hairlines instead of shadows, #3BB75E as the single accent. They differ in paper warmth, type pairing, and corner geometry. Each column shows the four signature components — a button cluster, a search input, a tutor list row, and a tutor profile card — so the difference is material, not theoretical.
