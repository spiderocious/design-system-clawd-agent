Marcus has been teaching A-Level Mathematics in Lekki for eight years. His students come up through King's College and Greensprings, and he sees roughly fifteen a year — small enough that he writes individually to every parent at the end of each term.

He keeps a Sunday note on the site where he explains one problem from the week in plain language: why it matters, where it shows up later, what a careful student would notice on first reading. Parents read these to their younger children at the kitchen table.
