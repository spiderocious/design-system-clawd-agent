# Original brief

> a simple product that enables filling station owners to manage it properly.
> they can own multiple branches, they can manage each branch
>
> you can figure out the features yourself (nigerian context)

## Clarifications during discovery

- **Primary user:** Both owner and manager, but owner wins ties.
- **Signature moment:** All of them — "managing staffs, managing entries, liter left, dip, stock management (based off manager's entries)." Importantly: **no hardware connections, no POS/transfer linking**. The system is a structured digital logbook.
- **Feel:** Confident modern SaaS, referenced against QuickBooks/Xero (ledger-serious), not Paystack (fintech-playful).
- **Brand colour:** Green-coded (fuel / Nigeria) — but deep emerald, not NNPC corporate-green or pump safety-green.
- **Daily entries:** Pump readings per shift · tank dip + deliveries · expenses & cash out · staff & shifts · price changes.

## Stance chosen

**A — Ledger broadsheet (Source Serif 4 + Inter + IBM Plex Mono, warm cream `#F2EDE2`, deep emerald `#0E5C3A`, hairlines, tight 3px geometry.)**

## Name

**Dipstick** — chosen by the user. Owns the most fraud-prone moment in the business; mechanic-coded; the wordmark reads well set in serif.
