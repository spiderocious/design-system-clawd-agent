Acuity — ESI 1 through 5.

Five levels, mono numerals. ESI-1 is ink with a critical underbar; the others step down into amber, green, paper.

Code status, isolation, risk — stamped marks.

These are not pills; they are stamps. Mono caps with generous tracking, often inverted. The kind of mark you'd find pressed onto a chart cover.

Allergy band — never dismissible.

A red mark across the chart, set in serif italic. The "ALLERGY" label is a mono stamp inside it. Different shades for advisory, precaution, anaphylactic.
