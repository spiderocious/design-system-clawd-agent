Clinical semantic colors

These are deliberately separate from the generic semantic ramp. Critical, abnormal-high, abnormal-low, normal, pending, and STAT each carry distinct meaning in clinical context. Red is reserved exclusively for life-threatening conditions to prevent alert fatigue.
