25 / DATA & STATE

Empty · loading · error

The off-states · still calm

Most products treat empty / loading / error as afterthoughts. In Tutorly they are scenes too — the parent reading "no tutors yet" is the parent we are most likely to lose. We write to them in plain language and offer one next step, not five.

Scene · loading · the search just submitted

Five row-shaped silhouettes

A skeleton that matches the row's exact shape. No spinning wheels, no "Loading…" text. The page feels mid-step, not stalled.

Scene · empty · no tutors yet

"We don't have anyone teaching Yoruba in Calabar — yet."

Empty is honest. We never invent fake results, we never tell the parent "try widening your search" without an action attached.

No tutors for Yoruba in Calabar — yet

We have 27 Yoruba tutors elsewhere in Nigeria. You can open the search to all of Nigeria, or we'll let you know the moment a Yoruba tutor joins in Calabar.

Scene · empty · inbox · before the first connect

The parent's empty inbox

After they sign in but before they've connected with anyone. The empty state explains the rule of the room.

Your conversations will appear here

When you connect with a tutor, the conversation opens here. Contact information stays hidden — Tutorly delivers every message until you both agree to share details.

Scene · error · payment failed

The connect-fee charge didn't go through

Errors are warm brick-red on cream — never alarming, always specific about what went wrong and what to do next.

Your card was declined
Your bank turned the ₦2,500 connection fee away. You can try a different card, or pay with bank transfer.
