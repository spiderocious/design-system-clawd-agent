Design rules · critical modals
  • Eyebrow names the step. Never "Are you sure?" — always "Step 3 of 3 · Confirm connection."
  • Title states the act. "Connect with Marcus K. for ₦2,500" beats "Confirm connection."
  • Receipt with heavy total rule. The parent sees the breakdown, mono and tabular. Total stands alone.
  • Acknowledge box, not a tick by default. Optional in the receipt-style above when the figure is small; mandatory for amounts > ₦10,000.
  • Action button restates the consequence in its label. "Connect — ₦2,750." Not "Confirm." Not "Yes, do it."
  • Cancel sits left, quiet variant. Never beside the action, never destructively styled.
  • Esc closes; Enter does not submit. The action requires a deliberate click.
