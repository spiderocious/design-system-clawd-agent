12 / PRIMITIVES

Selection

Checkbox · radio · toggle · pill

Selection controls borrow the brand green only when they're chosen. Otherwise they sit quietly in sheet-edge grey. The pill is the workhorse for filter chips — large enough to read, soft enough to feel like paper.

Scene · search filters · subjects

Pill-radio for "what do you want learned?"

A parent picks one subject at a time. Pills are friendly without being cartoonish; they read as printed labels, not buttons.

Scene · search filters · level

Multi-checkbox for "exam they're sitting"

A student may be preparing for two exams at once. Checked rows tint pale sage so the choice is felt, not just seen.

Scene · tutor settings · availability

Toggle for "show me in search results"

Used in tutor settings. Toggles never carry meaning beyond on/off — anything more complex becomes a radio set.

Show me in search results
When off, your profile is hidden from search. Existing conversations stay open.
Email me when a parent reads my profile
A daily digest, not per-view. We will never email you more than once a day.
